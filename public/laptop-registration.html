Step By Step Instructions On How To Register With Your PC Or Laptop And Start Earning Ethereum Daily With Forsage Decentralized Smart Contract Project!


How To Setup Metamask Wallet On Your Laptop


Step 1:

Install Metamask extension for Chrome of Firefox

Step 2:

Create a wallet by clicking the get started button and follow the instruction


Step 3:

Click the I agree button to continue wallet setup.


Step 4:

Create a strong password for your wallet and click the Create button.


Step 5:

Click the padlock to reveal the backup phrase, click the Next button. You can download or write down the generated 12-word backup phrase.


Step 6:

Confirm your secret backup phrase by typing in the 12-words phrase and clicking the confirm button.

Step 7:

Copy the generated receiving address


Step 8:

Top up your wallet balance by sending Ethereum to the address you copied in the step above. The entry fee is 0.05 ETH but it is recommended to get at least 3-4 slots in both of the Matrixes upon getting started. To do so after you register with 0.05 ETH, you upgrade to slots 3-4 in x3 and x4 matrixes. To unlock 3 slots in both matrixes you need about 0.37ETH (0.35ETH plus gas fees). To unlock 4 earning slots in both matrixes you need 0.77ETH (0.65ETH plus gas fees).
