Step By Step Instructions On How To Register With Your Smartphone And Start Earning Ethereum Daily With Forsage Decentralized Smart Contract Project!


How to Setup Trust Wallet On Your Smartphone


Step 1:

Install Trust Wallet DApp from Google Play Store or Apple App Store.

Step 2:

Open the app and click on the Create A New Wallet button.


Step 3:

Click on the checkbox and hit the Continue button to reveal your 12-word backup phrase.


Step 4:

Copy or write the generated 12-word recovery phrase and click the Continue button. It is very important that you securely store the 12-word passphrase. It will be needed in restoring your wallet and funds if your smartphone gets stolen or damaged.

Step 5:

Verify the 12-word recovery phrase by tapping the words in the order in which they were generated and click the Done button. Congrats! You successfully created your wallet.

Step 6:

Click on the Ethereum balance and select the Receive button.


Step 7:

Click the Copy button to copy the generated receiving address to your clipboard.


Step 8:

Replenish your wallet balance by sending Ethereum to the address you copied in the step above. The entry fee is 0.05 ETH but it is recommended to get at least 3-4 slots in both of the Matrixes upon getting started. To do so after you register with 0.05 ETH, you upgrade to slots 3-4 in x3 and x4 matrixes. To unlock 3 slots in both matrixes you need about 0.37ETH (0.35ETH plus gas fees). To unlock 4 earning slots in both matrixes you need 0.77ETH (0.65ETH plus gas fees).

You can purchase Ethereum from binance.com
